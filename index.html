New · Strategy RPG

Meow God's Maze · Doomsday Defense

The continent’s light has dimmed, leaving a single magical lighthouse as the final beacon. You are the guardian charged with defending it from relentless monster legions.

Meow God’s Maze fuses tower defense, strategy RPG depth, and roguelite randomness. Every run reshuffles hero lineups, relics, and threats, so on-the-fly planning truly determines survival.

Prep your formation by fortifying the lighthouse, training feline heroes, and drafting relics. Then switch to defense mode and unleash skills, gadgets, and emergency repairs to withstand 360° sieges until the BOSS falls.

Combat pillars

  • Adaptive roguelike waves: dynamic stages, mutators, and loot ensure every night feels fresh and dangerous.
  • Dual-phase strategy: meticulous prep decisions echo through real-time defense, rewarding foresight and quick reactions.
  • Mythic cat guardians: mix ranged snipers, melee tanks, and arcane casters to counter any legion.

Hold the line, rescue survivors, and guide the lighthouse into a new dawn. The maze awaits— are you ready to command the last hope?
