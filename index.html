New · Strategy RPG

Meow God's Maze · Doomsday Defense

Guard the final lighthouse through a deliberate cadence of planning, deployment, and furious last-stand defenses.

The darkness invades the world, and the light of the entire continent is swallowed. Somewhere in the world, a final magical lighthouse shines its last light. Defend it, the world's last hope!

Meow Tower - Doomsday Defense is a game rich in roguelike elements. Real-time changes and strategies during battles will affect the outcome of the fight. It is a strategic RPG that also incorporates roguelite random elements. You will play as a guardian, defending the lighthouse from the attack of monster legions.

In the preparation phase, you need to carefully consider how to build and upgrade the lighthouse, as well as arm and guide various heroes. Then, in the defense phase, you need to use your wisdom, various heroes, and props to repel enemies pouring in from all directions. Make your heroes stronger through battles, perform necessary recoveries in a timely manner, until you defeat the BOSS and successfully survive to a new day.
